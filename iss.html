ISS Science Research Project

Problem Statement

Effect of salt concentration of a buoyant force on a miniature ship.

Project Context

My group and I conducted research on how the salt concentration in water affects the buoyant force on a miniature ship. We chose this topic due to the rapid growth of the shopping industry following its halt during COVID-19. This research project aims to provide conclusive data on whether salt concentration influences the buoyancy force acting on ships during shipments.

Result Collection

We used a data logger connected to a salinity metre to collect the data on weather there will be a change in buoyant force acting on the miniature ship. We inputted the data into a spreadsheet and I created a graph to process the data that was collected.
